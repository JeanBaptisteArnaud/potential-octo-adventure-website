Opal Compiler

The OpalCompiler is a Smalltalk-to-Bytecode compiler for Pharo. Originaly it was named NewCompiler.

  • Support for full block closures.
  • Easy to understand and modify.
  • Based on the AST of the Refactoring Engine.
  • Retargable Backend

I was one of the initial developer of bytecode generator. Here is a presentation about OpalCompiler on DeepIntoPharo: link

OpalCompiler is now integrate into Pharo 3.0

Now Opal Compiler is maintain by Marcus Denkers et Bera Clement.
